ForeSite360

Large enterprise systems are deeply interconnected — when something breaks, teams scramble just to figure out where the problem is, let alone how to fix it. ForeSite360 was designed to change that.

Key Contributions

  • Lead Designer

Results

45%

faster incident resolution

30%

fewer support tickets initiated by users

Custom Dashboards

Enterprise teams are pulled in every direction. Through user interviews across every role, I found that each team was drowning in metrics that didn't apply to them. I designed role-specific dashboards that surface only what matters to each user's domain — cutting through the noise so they can focus on the decisions that actually require their attention.

Courses of Action

When something does go wrong, speed matters. I designed a system-wide dependency map that not only pinpoints root causes across complex infrastructure, but also surfaces suggested courses of action with projected outcomes — so teams spend less time diagnosing and more time deciding.

Automated Support Tickets

For the most critical issues, even a short delay is too long. Configurable alerts automatically submit help desk tickets the moment an anomaly is detected — so the right people are already working on a fix before most users even notice something is wrong.

Conclusion

ForeSite360 shifted teams from reactive firefighting to confident, proactive decision-making. By giving each user the right information at the right time — and automating the first response when it matters most — the experience reduced both the frequency and impact of critical system failures.
