Updated User Dashboard

A personalized dashboard that presented high-level data to the user upon sign-in, allowing them to start the 4 most common workflows including a section of recent activity so they could quickly pick back up where they left off.

Advanced Search

Previously, users had to search individual columns to narrow down their search. I designed an advanced search workflow based on the natural search processes the users were going through. When combined with our new bulk approval feature this resulted in a 75% increase in approval times for users.

Accessibility Enhancements

When the new design system was introduced, the color palette as well as many of the components were out of 508 compliance. I adjusted colors and provided alternative components to ensure accessibility.

Conclusion

I designed multiple new features, streamlining the user experience from log in to log out to bring their MVP to Go-Live with 2 Fortune 500 financial institutions.
